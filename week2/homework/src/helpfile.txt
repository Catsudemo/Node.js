Welcome to the To Do List CLI app. 
Here are the functions you can use in this app. 
Add - Type add plus the reminder you want to add to your list. E.g. add buy milk. 
Remove - Type remove plus the number of the reminder you want to remove from your list. E.g. remove 2.
List - Type list to read everything currently on your list. 
Reset - Type reset to remove everything from your list. Don't worry, you'll be asked to confirm before resetting.
